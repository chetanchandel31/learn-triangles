Types of triangles

  • A triangle having a right angle(90°) is called a right angled triangle.
  • A triangle with all angles measuring less than 90° is called an acute triangle.
  • A triangle with an angle measuring more than 90° is called an obtuse angle.

Sum of angles of a triangle

sum of all 3 angles of a triangle is always 180°.
